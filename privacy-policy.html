Overview

Abang Colek WOCS (WhatsApp Operations Command System) is a Chrome extension that enhances WhatsApp Web with CRM features. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

What we collect:
  • Contact Names & Numbers: Extracted from WhatsApp Web when you use the Export Contacts feature
  • Chat Labels: Custom labels you assign to chats
  • Message Templates: Templates you create or use
  • Usage Statistics: Count of messages sent (stored locally)

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's Storage API. We do NOT:

  • Send your data to external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Store your WhatsApp messages
  • Access your WhatsApp account credentials

Data Usage

Your data is used solely to:

  • Export contacts to CSV/JSON files on your device
  • Display chat labels within WhatsApp Web
  • Enable quick message templates
  • Generate wa.me links for bulk messaging

Third-Party Services

This extension interacts only with:

  • WhatsApp Web (web.whatsapp.com): For reading contact information and inserting messages

We do not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Permissions Used

  • storage: To save your preferences and labels locally
  • activeTab: To interact with WhatsApp Web when you click the extension
  • scripting: To inject CRM features into WhatsApp Web

Data Deletion

You can delete all extension data at any time by:

  1. Right-click the extension icon
  2. Select "Remove from Chrome"
  3. All locally stored data will be deleted

Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for users under 13 years of age.
